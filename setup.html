Setup helper. Do the Telegram steps here in your browser, then run one command on your receiver.

1

Create your Telegram bot

In Telegram, open @BotFather, send /newbot, follow the prompts, and copy the token it gives you (looks like 8123456789:AAH...).

2

Link it to you

Open your new bot in Telegram and press Start (or send it any message). Then click below.

3

Choose what to be alerted about

Adds the /status /spots /feed ... menu to your bot. Needs the token from step 1.

5

Install on your receiver

SSH into your OpenWebRX machine (Raspberry Pi, etc.) and paste this whole block. It installs everything with your settings above - no further questions.
